About Me

Dr. Pintu Paul is an Assistant Professor at the Indian Social Institute, New Delhi. His research focuses on family demography, migration, education, health, and gender issues. He holds an MPhil and a PhD in Geography from the Centre for the Study of Regional Development, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi. Dr. Paul previously worked at Ashoka University, the International Institute for Population Sciences (IIPS), and the Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS).

Dr. Paul has authored several research papers published in internationally reputed social science journals, including Social Science & Medicine, SSM-Population Health, Journal of Interpersonal Violence, and Economic & Political Weekly. He has also published op-eds in popular media outlets and newspapers, including Ideas for India, India Development Review, The Indian Express, and Down To Earth.
